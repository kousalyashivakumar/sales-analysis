Sales data analysis using Python to identify revenue trends, customer behavior, and key business insights
# Sales Data Analysis

## Objective
To analyze sales data and identify key drivers of revenue, customer behavior, and business performance.

## Tools & Technologies
Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Key Analysis
- Monthly sales trend analysis  
- Top-performing products and regions  
- Customer segmentation and repeat purchase analysis  
- Pareto analysis (80-20 rule) for revenue contribution  
- Identification of returns and their impact on sales  

## Key Insights
- A small percentage of customers contribute to a large portion of revenue  
- Certain products drive the majority of sales  
- Sales performance varies across regions  
- Customer retention plays a key role in business growth  

## Dataset
Retail sales dataset

## Conclusion
Focusing on high-value customers and optimizing product strategy can significantly improve revenue and profitability.
